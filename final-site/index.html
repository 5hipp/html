About

Are you releasing new music soon? Do you want to be featured on student-led music blog? Fancy an interview or a review of your music?
Music Business student Jemma Crane created Instagram blog Beyond the Melodies in 2020 and has worked with many bands, artists and musicians since. Jemma Crane has now created a brand new website for the page 'beyondthemelodies.com' a space which shares previous posts and brand new articles!
