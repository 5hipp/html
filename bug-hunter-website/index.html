How it Works

Bug Hunter offers companies a level of interaction no other bug bounty program can accomplish, with integrated Virtual Machines, our website allows for hands on work with industry level equipment directly within the browser, no need for downloads or any other software!

1

Companies Contact

Companies contact us to host live or development versions of their computer systems on our website in order for them to be stress tested, poked and prodded by a larger community of people.
2

Bug Searching

These uploaded software and systems are then hunted by the community to find bugs and security flaws ahead of deployment for the companies. 
3

Bug Rewards

These companies can put forward rewards in return for bugs and security related issues found within the software and systems they've uploaded.
4

Industry Results

By interacting with industry standard computer systems and software, you will build a predefined knowledge base of how companies in the industry work, meaning your applications and know how with these companies is larger than competitors, offering better job opportunities in the future.
